About

We are just a group of friends who love country and dancing. We got together in 2021 with a goal to create a christian square dance community for young adults. Our little backyard gig has grown to the point where we have connected with some of the best callers in the area.

We welcome any and all dancers who are interested in square dance. We have a variety of skill levels present at each event and we are always looking for new dancers to join our community.

"To create a christian square dance community for young adults."
